HERO IMAGES
===========

Drop your hero photos here. Name them:

  slide-1.jpg  →  Slide 1 (main hero)
  slide-2.jpg  →  Slide 2
  slide-3.jpg  →  Slide 3

Recommended:
- Size: 1200 × 900px or larger
- Format: JPG or WebP
- Subject: beneficiaries, training sessions, community activities, products

After adding photos, update the `image` field in:
  src/components/HeroCarousel.tsx
  → Set each slide's image to e.g. '/hero/slide-1.jpg'
